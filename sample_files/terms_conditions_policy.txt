INSURANCE POLICY TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. DEFINITIONS
"Policyholder" refers to the individual named in the policy schedule.
"Premium" means the amount payable by the policyholder for coverage.
"Coverage Period" means the duration for which the policy is active as stated in the schedule.
"Claim" means a formal request by the policyholder for payment under the policy.

2. COVERAGE
The insurer agrees to provide coverage as specified in the policy schedule, subject to the terms, conditions, and exclusions stated herein. Coverage is effective from the start date and expires on the end date listed in the policy schedule.

3. PREMIUM PAYMENT
Premiums are due on the date specified in the policy schedule. A grace period of 30 days is allowed for late payments. Failure to pay within the grace period will result in policy suspension.

4. EXCLUSIONS
The following are not covered under this policy:
- Pre-existing conditions diagnosed within 12 months prior to policy start
- Intentional self-inflicted injuries
- Acts of war or terrorism
- Participation in illegal activities
- Cosmetic or elective procedures not medically necessary

5. CLAIMS PROCEDURE
All claims must be filed within 90 days of the incident. The policyholder must provide supporting documentation including medical reports, police reports (if applicable), and proof of loss. Fraudulent claims will result in immediate policy cancellation and legal action.

6. RENEWAL
Policies are renewable annually. The insurer reserves the right to adjust premiums at renewal based on claims history and risk assessment. Policyholders will be notified 60 days prior to expiration.

7. CANCELLATION
The policyholder may cancel the policy at any time with written notice. A pro-rated refund will be issued for unused premium. The insurer may cancel the policy for non-payment, fraud, or material misrepresentation.

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes arising from this policy shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with applicable law.
